What is Kuara

Kuara is an end-to-end data solution for digital marketers looking to levarage their campaign data. It's a data lakehouse that extracts reports directly from their sources, and serves business owners with its curated insights.

Bruno Pini, founder of pinimartec, is a former growth marketer and now data engineer. He grew tired of the messy and coslty connector-as-a-service solutions available and dedicated his efforts on developing a more comprehensive and simpler solution.
