What is Kuara

Kuara is an end-to-end data solution for digital marketers looking to levarage their digital campaigns data. It is a data lakehouse that extracts reports firectly from the adtechs, and curates it before serving businesses owners with its insights.

Bruno Pini, founder of pinimartec, is a former growth marketer and now data engineer. He grew tired of dealing with bad data and the fragmented stack currently available, with costly subscriptions that provide no real value except when combined with other costly subscriptions.
